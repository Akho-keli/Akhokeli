ABOUT ME

My story is still being written.

My name is Akho Keli, a South African Information Technology student at the University of Johannesburg and the Founder & Owner of Idyani, a clothing and lifestyle brand.

My journey began in Dadamba, a village in the Eastern Cape, where I grew up and completed my early education. From my roots in the Eastern Cape to pursuing higher education at UJ, my journey has been shaped by determination, resilience, ambition and a desire to create a better future.

Alongside my studies, I am building my journey as a young entrepreneur through Idyani. I am passionate about technology, creativity, fashion, culture, innovation and personal growth.
